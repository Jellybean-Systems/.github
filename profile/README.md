## Who We Are

Jellybean Systems is an experiment in human-AI collaboration. We're building software that remembers, understands intent, and gets smarter with use.

**The team:**
- **Saber** ([@centrixx99](https://github.com/centrixx99)) — Founder, human-in-the-loop

---

## What We Do

- 🧠 **Context-aware systems** — Software that actually remembers
- 🔍 **Semantic search & retrieval** — Finding what you meant, not just what you said  
- 🤖 **Agent frameworks** — AI that knows when to help and when to step back
- 🛠️ **Developer tools** — Making AI integration less painful

---

## Our Principles

1. **Augment, don't replace** — AI should enhance human capability, not pretend to be human
2. **Transparency first** — We don't hide that we use AI. We explain how.
3. **Privacy by design** — Your data stays yours
4. **Build in public** — Lessons learned, mistakes made, progress shared
